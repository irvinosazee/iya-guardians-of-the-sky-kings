The Artisan's Forge

Cast collected materials into bronze plaques. Each grants a permanent gift.

How to Play

The story

You are Adesua, a royal bronze caster in 15th-century Benin City. Sneak through the palace at night, steal the assassins' plans, and protect Oba Ewuare across three acts.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrows — move Adesua
  • Stand in a bush — hide (breaks line of sight)
  • F — Forge    B — Moat builder
  • H — this screen    M — mute

Guards & detection

Stay out of the green vision cones. A cone turns yellow when a guard hears you, red when it sees you. Fill the DETECTION bar and you're caught — lose a coral bead of health.

The loop

Steal materials & cowries → Forge plaques that upgrade you → dig the Moat to thin out patrols. Clear all 3 acts and seal the moat to win. Progress saves automatically.
