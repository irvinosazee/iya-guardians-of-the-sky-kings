How to Play

The story

You are Adesua, a royal bronze caster in 15th-century Benin City. Sneak through the palace at night, steal the assassins' plans, and protect Oba Ewuare across three acts.

Controls

  • WASD move · drag look · scroll zoom
  • Shift sprint (loud) · C crouch (quiet)
  • E throw a distraction · bush = hide
  • F Forge · B Moat · Esc Pause · M mute

Guards & detection

Stay out of the green vision cones. They turn yellow when a guard hears you, red when seen. The white ring shows how far your steps carry. Fill DETECTION and you're caught.

The loop

Steal materials & cowries → Forge upgrades → dig the Moat to thin patrols. Clear 3 acts and seal the moat to win. Progress saves automatically.
