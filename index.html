The Artisan's Forge

Cast collected materials into bronze plaques. Each grants a permanent gift.

How to Play

The story

You are Adesua, a royal bronze caster in 15th-century Benin City. Sneak through the palace at night, steal the assassins' plans, and protect Oba Ewuare across three acts.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrows — move Adesua
  • Stand in a bush — hide
  • F — Forge   B — Moat   Esc — Pause
  • H — help   M — mute

Guards & detection

Stay out of the green vision cones. They turn yellow when a guard hears you, red when seen. The white ring around you shows how far your footsteps carry. Fill DETECTION and you're caught.

The loop

Steal materials & cowries → Forge plaques that upgrade you → dig the Moat (the glowing ring) to thin out patrols. Clear all 3 acts and seal the moat to win. Progress saves automatically.
